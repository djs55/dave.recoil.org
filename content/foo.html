My current interests lie in
  • systems, especially operating system design
  • security, especially at the application-level
  • ubiquitous computing
  • user interfaces
  • networking
Past projects include:
  • The Ubiquitous Interaction Devices (UID) project encompassing
    • SpotCode Interfaces
    • Audio Networking
    • Bluetooth-based Mobile Services
  • Location-aware computing including
    • Analysing participation in voluntary location systems
    • Location-aware gaming
  • Security
    • Application-level policies for web-applications
    • Spatial policies for restricting mobility
My PhD research focused on application-level security policies for mobile web-based systems. The work was supervised by Alan Mycroft in the CL. I'm grateful to Andy Hopper from the LCE and the Schiff Foundation for support and funding respectively. I am also sponsored by the now defunct AT&T Laboratories Cambridge Ltd.
