I'm currently working for Citrix, making virtualisation products based on the open source Xen hypervisor. Perviously I was a consultant research scientist at Fraser Research, where I worked on their next-generation networking initiatives. In 2004 I finished a PhD in the Lab for Comms Engineering (LCE) (now known as the "Digital Technology Group")in the University of Cambridge Computer Laboratory affiliated with the Cambridge Programming Research Group . I was a co-founder of High Energy Magic, which produced mobile phone software to recognise printed visual symbols, known as SpotCodes
